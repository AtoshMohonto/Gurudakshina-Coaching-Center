About Us

GuruDokkhin Coaching Center is a human-centric, value-based education initiative. It is designed for SSC-level and underprivileged students who need academic recovery, confidence building, and ethical guidance.

Our Principles

  • Truth and ethical learning
  • No student denied due to financial limitations
  • Discipline and commitment over money
  • Mutual respect between teachers and students

Programs

  • SSC Special Care Batch
  • English from Zero
  • Basic Math Recovery
  • ICT & Computer Basics

Fee Model

We follow a Guru-Dokkhinā-based system. Students contribute according to their ability. Those who cannot pay financially contribute through discipline, effort, and sincerity.
